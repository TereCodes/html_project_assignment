Rice and Stew

The Nigerian White Rice and Stew is a popular Nigerian rice recipe because it lets you eat rice with any stew or sauce of your choice. This rice recipe is usually eaten with Beef and/or Chicken Stew which we almost always have in the freezer so it is just a matter of boiling white rice. So if you already have Beef/Chicken Stew in your freezer, the Boiled White Rice and Stew will be one easy dinner recipe to make if you are tired after a long busy day.

Ingredients needed

Ingredients measurements
beef meat 1 kg
fresh tomatoes 1/2 kg
scotch bonnet pepper (ata rodo) 1/2 cup
red bell peppers (tatashe) 4
curry 3tb spoon
thyme 3tb spoons
knorr seasoning cubes 4 cubes
curry leaf/parsley 2-3 leafs
onion 2 medium size
vegetable oil 1 cup
salt to taste from 1tb spoon
  1. blend tomatoes,curry leaf/parsley,onion,pepper
  2. bring a pan or pot to low or medium heat
  3. add vegetable oil
  4. pour your blended tomatoes and pepper
  5. add your seasonings
  6. add already boiled beef and/or broth,then stir
  7. allow to cook for 20-30 mins
  8. parboil rice,wash and cook to done in another pot
